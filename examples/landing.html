Built for designers and agencies

Slidus is designed from the ground-up to support and enhance the workflow of web designers. It’s more than just a slogan for us.


Smooth workflows
Create accounts with limited interaction from your clients. Send them a link to enter their payment info, and they’re good to go.
A single login
Access all of your clients’ sites with a single FTP login. No need to remember 50 different usernames and passwords.
Demo sites are free
Easily test out changes and preview them for your clients, before publishing them to the live site.
Earn recurring income
Make money each month for the clients you host with Slidus. No need to pass around affiliate links or referral codes.
